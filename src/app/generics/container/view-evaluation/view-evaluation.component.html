Principles, values, and Team synergy
  1. Team participation: During the initiative, did you actively involve and engage all team members, ensuring their meaningful participation and contributions?
  2. Transparency: Is the initiative's progress and results communicated openly and transparently to stakeholders? Are there any potential conflicts of interest that may impact transparency?
  3. Accountability: Is there a clear system in place for holding individuals and teams accountable for their actions and results? Are individuals and teams held responsible for any negative consequences resulting from their actions?
  4. Collaboration: Does the initiative encourage collaboration and teamwork to achieve its goals? Are there any instances of conflicts or tensions among team members or stakeholders?
  5. Efficiency: Is the initiative designed to maximize efficiency and minimize waste in the pursuit of its objectives?
  6. Effectiveness: Are resources being used effectively to achieve the initiative's goals?
  7. Equity: Does the initiative consider the needs of all stakeholders and ensure equitable outcomes? Are there any potential biases or inequalities that need to be addressed?
  8. Flexibility: Is the initiative flexible enough to adapt to changing circumstances and challenges? Are there any potential barriers to flexibility, such as rigid timelines or budget constraints?
  9. Sustainability: Does the initiative prioritize long-term sustainability and consider the environmental and social impact of its actions? Are there any potential negative environmental or social consequences that need to be addressed?
  10. Innovation: Does the initiative encourage innovation and creativity in problem-solving and achieving its objectives? Are there any potential opportunities for innovation that have not been fully explored?
